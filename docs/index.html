FAQ

Where does the flight data come from?

All data is sourced from the City of Minnetonka's public transparency portal. It is public record.

Is this real-time?

Flight data is typically available within hours of a mission ending. It is not live or real-time.

How do alerts work?

Save locations you care about (home, school, workplace) and get push notifications when drone flights occur nearby. Premium subscription required for alerts.

Is this an official city app?

No. This is an independent project by CJC Digital LLC. It is not affiliated with any government agency.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open the Settings app on your iPhone → tap your name → Subscriptions → Minnetonka Drone Tracker → Cancel.

How do I delete my data?

Use the support form above with the topic "Privacy / Data Deletion" and we will process your request within 30 days.
